WOMEN

In Tech

When it comes to women's participation in the workforce, the country has come a long way in the last few decades. According to a Catalyst survey, women account for nearly half of the workforce (46.9%). Despite all of women's achievements, the tech industry does not seem to be a happy place for them. Women have had a hard time finding jobs in the software industry and other STEM fields for decades. In the STEM fields, there is a significant gender disparity between men and women. This disparity expresses itself in a variety of ways, including job prospects, salaries, leadership opportunities, organisational perception, and so on.
